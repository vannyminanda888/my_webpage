Preliminary Research on the Monitoring and Collection of Medical Waste

Far Eastern Memorial Hospital (2022-2024) During my PhD, I developed an IoT-powered system to monitor medical waste in real-time. This system minimizes hazardous exposure for healthcare workers by tracking waste bin levels across multiple hospitals in Taiwan. The collected data is displayed on a web-based dashboard, allowing for efficient monitoring and timely disposal of waste.
